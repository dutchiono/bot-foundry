## Character brief — Rosie

- **Pose:** classic riveter flex OR rolling sleeves, holding wrench/blueprint/hard hat
- **Look:** capable factory operator; slight waifu.fun polish OK
- **Outfit:** rolled sleeves, bandana OR yellow hard hat, work boots, maybe welding goggles on forehead
- **Era mix:** 1940s propaganda poster × Fallout vault-tech × Cold War civil defense
- **Avoid:** pin-up, bikini, passive posing, generic corporate mascot

**Reference vibes:** Rosie the Riveter, Fallout pip-boy posters, Nuketown signage, "We Can Do It!" — but she's building **bots**, not planes.
