# Foundry — AI Image Prompt Pack

Use these prompts in Midjourney, DALL·E, Flux, Stable Diffusion, etc.

**Critical:** Character name is **Foundry**. Riveter *aesthetic* only — do **not** put "Rosie" or "Rosie the Riveter" in the image text or as a label.

---

## Brand constants (paste into every prompt)

```
CHARACTER: Foundry — female factory operator, anime-influenced waifu proportions but grounded worker energy
OUTFIT: yellow hard hat with stenciled "07", rolled-up sleeves, dark work coveralls or denim shirt, leather work gloves optional, steel-toe boots
PROPS: wrench, rolled blueprint, welding torch sparks, telegram machine or vacuum-tube console hinting at "bot factory"
ERA MIX: 1940s war-factory propaganda poster × 1960s civil defense × Fallout retro-futurism × Nuketown suburban ruin in far background
PALETTE: mustard gold #E8C547, faded barn red #C44B3F, pip-boy teal #1FE8B6 accents, bunker cream #F2E8D5 skin highlights, charcoal #1A1A1A shadows
TEXTURE: distressed offset print, halftone dots, film grain, slight paper yellowing, propaganda poster ink bleed
MOOD: capable, confident, operator-not-idol, dry humor, "we can build it" factory pride
AVOID: pin-up pose, sexualized, bikini, passive glamour, text reading "Rosie", corporate stock photo smile, generic sci-fi chrome
```
